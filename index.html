ABOUT NICK

My name is Nick Long, and I'm a 20-year-old sports photographer from Sevierville, Tennessee. I picked up a camera at 16 and quickly fell in love with capturing the energy, emotion, and split-second moments that make sports unforgettable.

Since then, I've had the opportunity to cover many SEC basketball games and the University of Tennessee's Orange & White Game — experiences that have pushed me to grow both creatively and professionally.

For me, sports photography is more than action shots — it's about telling the story of the game through every frame.
